1. Knee-to-chest · 60s each side
Pull knee to chest then across toward opposite shoulder. Directly targets piriformis.
2. Figure-4 stretch · 60s each side
Cross right ankle over left knee, push right knee away, pull left thigh toward chest. RIGHT SIDE FIRST.
3. 90/90 hip stretch · 60s each side
Both legs at 90° on floor. Sit tall. Towel under hip if needed.
4. Kneeling hip flexor · 45s each side

6. Donkey kicks + fire hydrants · 10 each
End with glute activation. Stretch → activate. Every session, every day.
