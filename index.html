Body recomposition — the RASA approach
At 57 you are not trying to get light. You are trying to get lean and strong simultaneously. The creatine protects muscle. The 135–150g protein daily rebuilds it. The Zone 2 volume burns fat without eating muscle. The gym builds the posterior chain.

Do not diet aggressively. Eat enough to train hard. The weight comes off as a byproduct of volume — exactly what happened naturally from 90–92kg down to 84kg over three years. Trust the process. Let training do the work.

Morning hip routine — daily
1. Knee-to-chest · 60s each side
Pull knee to chest then across toward opposite shoulder. Right side first. Targets piriformis directly.
2. Figure-4 stretch · 60s each side
Right ankle over left knee, push right knee away, pull left thigh toward chest. RIGHT SIDE FIRST. Most targeted piriformis stretch.
3. 90/90 hip stretch · 60s each side
Both legs at 90° on floor. Sit tall. Towel under hip if needed.
4. Kneeling hip flexor · 45s each side
Kneeling lunge, rear knee on floor. Shift weight forward. Tuck pelvis under to deepen.
5. Cat-cow + thoracic rotation
10 cat-cows then one hand behind head, rotate elbow to ceiling × 8 each side.
6. Donkey kicks + fire hydrants · 10 each
End with glute activation after stretching. Stretch → activate. Every single morning.
